Purpose and Users

Provide easy access to global happiness information through interactive, web-hosted visualization dashboards tool that utilize data from the World Happiness Report (WHR). Intended users include individuals considering different countries in the world, NGOs and nonprofits identifying where to direct support and Governments looking to improve life for their constituents.

Data Source

We primarily utilize data from the World Happiness Report. This team provides comprehensive annual reports on “the state of happiness in the world today”. Our project seeks to recreate, add to and improve upon visualizations in the World Happiness Report and website. In addition to data from their website, we utilize age group level data kindly provided to us upon request by their Production Editor.
